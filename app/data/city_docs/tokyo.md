# Tokyo Travel Overview

Tokyo is organized around distinct neighborhoods. Asakusa is strong for temples, traditional culture, Senso-ji, food streets, and photography. Akihabara is useful for anime, manga, gaming, arcades, electronics, and themed cafes. Shibuya and Shinjuku are strong for nightlife, city views, shopping, and urban photography. Ueno combines parks, museums, gardens, and cultural attractions. Tsukiji Outer Market is useful for food-focused travelers and market photography.
