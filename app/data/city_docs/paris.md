# Paris Travel Overview

Paris is organized around museums, historic neighborhoods, gardens, food streets, and viewpoints. The Louvre is central for museums, art, history, and indoor culture. Montmartre is useful for photography, cafes, hilltop views, artists, and neighborhood walks. The Eiffel Tower and Trocadero provide classic architecture and skyline photos. The Latin Quarter is good for food, history, bookshops, and cultural wandering. Luxembourg Gardens supports relaxed nature time.
