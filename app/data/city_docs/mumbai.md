# Mumbai travel overview

Mumbai is a dense coastal city with strong food, culture, history, nightlife, shopping, and photography options. Colaba and Fort are useful first-day bases for Gateway of India, colonial architecture, Kala Ghoda galleries, CSMVS Museum, cafes, and heritage walks. Marine Drive and Girgaum Chowpatty work well for sunset, sea views, street snacks, and relaxed evening plans.

For culture and history, travelers can combine Elephanta Caves by ferry with South Mumbai heritage areas, or choose indoor museum time around Kala Ghoda during hot or rainy weather. Bandra West adds cafes, boutiques, street art, Bandstand promenade, nightlife, and coastal photography. Mumbai traffic can be heavy, so a 2-day itinerary should cluster activities by neighborhood and avoid too many cross-city transfers.
