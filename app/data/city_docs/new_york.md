# New York Travel Overview

New York offers museums, parks, food halls, skyline viewpoints, nightlife, theater, and photography. The Metropolitan Museum of Art is a major indoor museum for art and history. Central Park is strong for nature, relaxed walks, and skyline photos. Times Square and Broadway are useful for nightlife, theater, and bright urban photography. Chelsea Market is an indoor food and shopping stop. Brooklyn Bridge and DUMBO provide skyline views and waterfront walking.
